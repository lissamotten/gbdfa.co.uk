OUR MISSION

s

Success

We aspire to achieve consistent medal success at the Paralympic Games and other international events.

Awareness

Creating awareness of disability football within Great Britain and Northern Ireland

Independence

Become an independent organisation with excellent governance structures and systems.

Inspiration

We focus in inspiring and guiding the next generation of international and Paralympic medal winners.

 

OUR GOALS

“Delivering and supporting deliberate, systemic, consistent and repeatable success in international and Paralympic football”
